Everything you need to know about becoming a Digital Sahukar

Digital Sahukar is a platform that enables traditional lenders (Sahukars) from any part of India to run their lending business in a professional and legally compliant way through an NBFC-P2P (Non-Banking Financial Company-Peer to Peer) model. It digitizes the entire lending process while maintaining the personal relationships that traditional lending is known for.

Our platform provides an easy-to-use interface where you can request and receive a borrower's CIBIL score with their consent. This helps you make informed lending decisions based on their credit history and repayment capacity. The entire process is digital, secure, and compliant with all regulatory requirements.

Our platform includes a comprehensive tracking system that provides real-time updates on all your borrowers' repayments. You'll receive notifications for upcoming, completed, and missed payments. The dashboard gives you a complete overview of your lending portfolio, with detailed reports on each borrower's repayment history and current status.

We charge a small percentage fee on successful disbursal of loans. There are no setup costs, monthly subscriptions, or hidden charges. Our fee structure is transparent and designed to ensure that we succeed only when you succeed. For detailed pricing information, please contact our team.
